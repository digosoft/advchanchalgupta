What types of cases does Advocate Chanchal Gupta handle?
+
She specializes in all family law matters including divorce, child custody, maintenance and alimony, domestic violence, matrimonial property disputes, restitution of conjugal rights, and general family law consultations.
How long does a divorce case typically take?
+
Mutual consent divorce can be completed in 6–18 months. Contested divorce may take longer depending on complexity and court schedule. She will give you an honest assessment during consultation.
Is the initial consultation free and confidential?
+
Yes, absolutely. The initial consultation is completely confidential. Everything shared is protected by attorney-client privilege. You can speak openly without any worry.
Can I get interim maintenance during the case?
+
Yes. You can apply for interim maintenance under Section 24 of the Hindu Marriage Act or Section 125 CrPC. Courts can grant interim relief quickly to ensure financial support during the case.
What documents should I bring to the first meeting?
+
Bring your marriage certificate, Aadhaar cards, photos, any existing court orders, property/bank documents if relevant, and any written communication with your spouse. Don't worry if incomplete.
Does she take cases from outside Khatima?
+
Yes, she accepts clients from across Uttarakhand and surrounding regions for matters handled at Khatima Family Court. Please call to discuss jurisdiction for your specific matter.
